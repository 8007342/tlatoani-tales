# S1-900 — Logs are ingredients

Raw logs aren't truth. They're material for it. A log line is a grain; observability is the loaf you bake from many grains.

## Aha moment

Reading one log line and calling it "the truth" is tasting flour and calling it bread. The truth is in the *shape* a stream of logs takes over time. Logs are the ingredients; observability is the cooking.
