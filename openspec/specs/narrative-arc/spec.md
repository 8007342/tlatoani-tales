# Narrative Arc

## Purpose

The dramatic shape of the season. Sits above `concept-curriculum` (which is the *what*) and describes the *feel* — how Covi's confidence changes, when Tlatoāni reveals more of themselves, where the emotional beats land.

## Season 1: "From Vibes to Convergence"

### Covi's arc

- **Acts 1–3 (TT 01/15–04/15)**: Confident chaos. Covi thinks they're doing great. Each strip they're gently surprised. Humor comes from the gap between their confidence and reality.
- **Acts 4–7 (TT 05/15–08/15)**: Structural awakening. Covi starts asking questions. Humor shifts from "Covi is wrong" to "Covi is trying" — we laugh *with* them now.
- **Acts 8–13 (TT 09/15–13/15)**: Fluency. Covi starts recognizing patterns before Tlatoāni names them. Tlatoāni begins to smile more, speak less. `S1-1000` (dashboards-must-add-observability, strip TT 10/15) sits between S1-900 and S1-1100 in the arc and serves as the emotional/structural pivot into the deepening observability arc — the moment Covi's first proud artefact (a dashboard) is gently revealed to still be a louder log, and the arc commits to what observability actually *adds*.
- **Finale (TT 14/15)**: Covi *teaches the reader* by asking the AI a phrase. Tlatoāni is present but quiet. The reader realizes they could ask the same thing.
- **Coda (TT 15/15)**: a skeptic voice enters off-panel — "You can't apply this anywhere." Covi answers cheerfully: "Of course you can! This comic was!" Panel 3 shows the repo URL rendered as a small scroll in Tlatoāni's notebook. This strip is the proof by self-reference: the artefact teaches by existing.

### Tlatoāni's arc

- **Begins**: serene, reactive. Opens the umbrella, hands over the notebook.
- **Middle**: proactive but still wordless-leaning. Rotates the treadmill. Hands the telescope.
- **End**: barely speaks. The tools Tlatoāni introduced do the teaching. This is on purpose — wisdom transferred is wisdom that doesn't need a teacher present.

### Tonal rules

- Covi **never** becomes sad or discouraged. Every panel 3 leaves Covi slightly happier, not defeated. "Compassionate mockery" means the *situation* is funny, not the character.
- Tlatoāni **never** smug-lectures. The few words used should read as observation, not correction.
- No fourth-wall breaks until TT 14/15.

## Continuity

- Strips are standalone jokes but **consequential** — what Covi learned in TT 03/15 persists in TT 04/15's Covi.
- Recurring props appear earlier than their "teaching strip" to prime the reader (e.g. the notebook is in TT 01/15's background).
- Call-backs reward re-reading. Strip 12/15 may reference the exact composition of strip 02/15.
